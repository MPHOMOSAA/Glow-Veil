Our Story

The soul
behind the veil.

Glow Veil was started in 2023 in Sandton, Johannesburg, with a vision to create skincare that feels luxurious, gentle, and effective. The brand was built around one simple idea: every person deserves to feel confident in their natural glow.

Our products are designed with a soft, elegant approach to beauty. Glow Veil is more than skincare — it is a self-care experience made for people who want to feel beautiful, polished, and confident every day.

Our Foundation

What Drives Us

Our Mission

To create premium skincare that helps people feel soft, radiant, and confident in their own skin — every single day.

Our Vision

To become a trusted South African skincare brand known for luxury, softness, and natural radiance across the continent.

Our Aesthetic

Soft. Elegant. Feminine. Clean. Luxurious. Every detail of Glow Veil is designed with intention and beauty in mind.

Our Roots

Born in Sandton, Johannesburg in 2023 — Glow Veil represents modern South African beauty with a soft luxury touch.
